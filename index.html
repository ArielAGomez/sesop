ABOUT US

Our Story


The Secondary Energy Special Opportunities Partnership (SESOP) has been established to make equity and equity related investments in two areas of the alternative energy value chain in South Africa, namely solar and wind power. With the dynamics of the energy sector changing rapidly over the past few years, this has led to amazing opportunities in the alternative energy space. The Partnership has a strong, agile and well-balanced team consisting of seasoned executives, industry experts and investment professionals, with a combined career spanning over 140 years. Over time, the Partnership expects to bolster its team further.

SERVICES

International Projects

Whilst the concept of secondary investments in alternative energy projects is new in South Africa, internationally, the investment thesis for this asset class has been proven extensively. During each stage of the asset life cycle, international investors have found favour for their particular investment style, be that sustainable investments generally, green investments particularly, venture capital, real assets, corporate social investing, value or yield investments. These assets have proven to be highly attractive and yields have consistently come down as investors have shown considerable appetite for these assets, thereby increasing the valuations.

Other Services

SESOP have secured innovative funding for solar PV projects across Africa, enabling many projects to achieve operational phase where previously this was not possible, due to restrictions on pre-construction funding by commercial banks. We are able to finance projects 100% on-balance-sheet through to Commercial Operations, at which time they are de-risked, allowing SESOP to raise debt funding from international banks. This enables many projects to actually proceed, whereas previously this restriction would prove a huge obstacle. SESOP are also able to provide capital to fund local equity ownership within projects, alongside our ownership stake, thereby uplifting and empowering our local partners. We can therefore:

  • Provide full project funding through to operational phase;
  • Raise senior debt from international banks;
  • Finance the equity portions of local shareholders;
  • Provide a full project management role of the project development and funding; and
  • Act as a long-term partner and investor in the project.
